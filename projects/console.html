FU-GEN Console

3D Visualization / Product Design / Blender 3D
October 2021
FU-GEN is a concept design for a Handheld Gaming Console based on the Android Operating System, for a South African Start-up formally called P.A.S . The console was originally planned to be a modified Android Device with a Snapdragon Chipset. The project was cancelled after a few months of R&D. Though the idea for it still remains to exist.
